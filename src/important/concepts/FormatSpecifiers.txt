Format Specifiers :-

    (String & Character)
        1) String                          :-     %s
        2) Character                       :-     %c

    (Integer Types)
        1) Decimal (base 10)               :-     %d
        2) Octal (base 8)                  :-     %o
        3) Hax (lowercase)                 :-     %x
        4) Hax (uppercase)                 :-     %X

    (Floating Point)
        1) Decimal Float                   :-     %f
        2) Scientific Notation             :-     %e
        3) Scientific (uppercase)          :-     %E
        4) Shorter of %f or %e             :-     %g
        5) Hex float (advanced)            :-     %a

    (General / Miscellaneous)
        1) Boolean                         :-     %b
        2) Platform-Independent newline    :-     %n
        3) Literal '%'                     :-     %%
