OOPs Terminology :-

1) Abstraction :-
    Hiding Internal Details

2) Encapsulation :-
    The act of putting components together(in a capsule).
    In Java, Encapsulation simply means that the sensitive data can be hidden from users.

3) Inheritance :-
    The act of deriving new things from existing things.

4) Polymorphism :-
    One entity many forms.
