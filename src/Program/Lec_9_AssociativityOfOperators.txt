Precedence of Operators :-
    The operators are applied and evaluated based on precedence. For example (+,-) has less precedence compared to (*,/). Hence * & / are evaluated first. In case, we like to change this order, we can use parathesis.


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Associativity :-
    Associativity tells the direction of execution of operators. It can either be Left to Right or Right to Left.

    * /      --->  Left to Right
    + -      --->  Left to Right
    ++ =     --->  Right to Left
