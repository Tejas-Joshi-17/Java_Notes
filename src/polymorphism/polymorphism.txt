Java Polymorphism :-

    1) Polymorphism allows us to perform a single action in different ways.
    2) In other words, polymorphism allow you to define one interface and have multiple implementation.
    3) The word 'poly' means many and 'morphs' means forms, So it means many forms.

    There are two types of Polymorphism :-

        a) Compile-time Polymorphism :-    Method Overloading

        b) Runtime Polymorphism  :-  Method Overriding
